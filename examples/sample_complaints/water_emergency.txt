URGENT: Water Main Break - Immediate Action Required

Emergency Report - Water Department

Location: Corner of Elm Street and 3rd Avenue
Time of Discovery: 6:30 AM, March 25, 2024
Reported by: Emergency Services

SITUATION:
A major water main has burst at the intersection of Elm Street and 3rd Avenue, causing significant flooding and water service disruption to the entire downtown area.

IMMEDIATE IMPACTS:
- Complete water outage affecting 500+ businesses and 2,000+ residents
- Street flooding (approximately 3 feet deep in some areas)
- Traffic completely blocked on both Elm Street and 3rd Avenue
- Potential damage to underground electrical systems
- Several businesses in flood zone may need evacuation

SAFETY CONCERNS:
- Risk of electrical hazards due to water contact with power lines
- Unstable road conditions - possible sinkhole formation
- Contamination risk to remaining water supply
- Emergency vehicles cannot access the area

URGENT ACTIONS NEEDED:
1. Immediate water main shutoff to stop flooding
2. Emergency pumping to remove standing water
3. Electrical safety assessment and power shutoff if necessary
4. Road closure and traffic rerouting
5. Alternative water supply for affected residents
6. Structural assessment of nearby buildings

This is a CRITICAL emergency requiring immediate response from multiple departments including Water, Electricity, Public Works, and Emergency Services.
